M&T or “Multinational and Trends” is an Indonesian multinational clothing company that has been developing and exploring new fashion trends. We’re sells men’s fashionwear that are up to the current fashion trend. We always make our products to be comfortable, suit the situation, and catchy. We are not just follow the trends but we make the trend.

History
